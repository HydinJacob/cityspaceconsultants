STRUCTURAL ENGINEERING

"Our work is not merely concerning sensible design; it's about how sensible design enhances the well-being of individuals and makes a positive influence on society at the giant."

We have in depth expertise in the design, administration, and supervision of structural engineering projects within the non-public and public sectors in UAE. Our projects include residential, corporate, commercial, public, education and health-related building structures and villas. Our services span conceptual and feasibility planning to design detail and construction management. We are expertise in residential building structures, industrial building structures, high-rise structures, and in this field additionally provide specialist expertise in seismic design and wind engineering. We are well recognized for our cost efficient & sustainable structural design of the delivered projects.

Our extremely talented structural engineers combine an in-depth understanding of the properties and behavior of materials with modern analytical tools, including computer-aided design (CAD), structure modelling packages, building information modelling (BIM) and project designing methodologies. City Space has advanced the utilization of 3D building modelling to design and test structural solutions virtually, which will conjure images of complex geometry. This enables us to manage challenges like difficult site conditions, complex foundations and also the interface with existing infrastructure and neighbouring structures to make sure structural efficiency, optimum utilization and to form any structure the most effective it can be.

Virtual modelling helps engineers, architects and clients see how all components of a design work together. Seeing that, we are able to optimize structural efficiency. We can also overlay the essential factors of cost and time to understand the implications of decisions for project viability and sustainability.

Our client's businesses put great demands on their buildings, whether or not high-rise buildings, industrial buildings, residential buildings, public buildings or villas. We have a tendency to respond by improving the structural performance of our designs in ways in which meet clients’ goals. We ensure that our structural engineers work closely with clients and collaborators and stay up to date with industry issues so they will pioneer in response to real-world challenges and constraints. Working hand in hand with the client and all members of the design team, we tend to ensure a smooth passage through the statutory and legislative processes to deliver sophisticated, cost-effective, sustainable structural solutions aligned to our clients’ necessities and the architectural aspirations for their buildings.
